MultiFOLD – Results Help Guide

Use this guide to interpret prediction confidence, colouring, and scores.

Key Information

Chain Coloring

  • Blue: Accurate prediction
  • Red: Low confidence

Prediction Quality (pLDDT)

  • 90–100: Very high confidence
  • 70–90: High confidence
  • 50–70: Medium confidence
  • <50: Low confidence

pTM Score

Indicates confidence in the overall predicted structure. Values closer to 1.0 are more reliable.

Assembly Score

Reflects confidence in inter-chain interactions and structure assembly.

Interface Score

Indicates confidence in predicted protein–protein interfaces.

FAQ

What does a high pTM score mean?

A value above 0.8 suggests a reliable predicted structure.

Why are some regions red?

Red regions indicate low confidence predictions (pLDDT < 50).

How should Assembly Score be interpreted?

Lower scores suggest uncertainty in chain positioning.
